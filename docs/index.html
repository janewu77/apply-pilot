How it works

Three steps, zero frustration

No complicated setup. Fill your profile once — Apply Pilot handles the scanning, matching, and filling.

👤
1

Save your profile

Enter your personal info, work history, education, links, and Q&A presets once. Everything lives locally in your browser.

🔍
2

Scan the form

Open any job application page. Click the 🚀 icon or press Alt+F. Apply Pilot scans and matches fields in seconds.

✅
3

Fill and submit

Review colour-coded labels, click Fill High-Confidence, check the results, and submit. You stay in control.

Features

Everything you need to apply faster

Keyword matching, AI fallback, auto-learning, and always private.

🎨

Colour-coded labels

Every field gets a status label so you know exactly what happened at a glance — no guessing, no surprises.

Full Name — Jane Wu 🟢 Filled
LinkedIn Profile 🔵 Matched
Why do you want to join? 🟡 Manual
⚡

Instant keyword matching

Apply Pilot reads field labels, placeholders, and names — then maps them to your profile in milliseconds. No API call needed for the common stuff.

🤖

AI semantic matching optional

Connect your own Claude or GPT API key to handle edge-case fields and draft answers to open-ended questions like "Why do you want this role?" Your key stays on your device — nothing is collected.

📚

Auto-learning

Fill a yellow field manually, click Save to Presets, and Apply Pilot remembers it. Hit a similar question next time — it fills automatically. The extension gets smarter with every application.

🌍

Three languages, one extension

Keyword matching works natively across English, German, and Chinese application forms. Applying in London, Berlin, or Taipei? Apply Pilot reads the form correctly in every language.
